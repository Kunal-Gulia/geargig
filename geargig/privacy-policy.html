Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes how GearGig Technology Private Limited, having its registered office at Greater Noida West, India (“GearGig”, “we”, “us”, or “our”), collects, uses, stores, processes, and discloses your personal data when you access or use our website www.geargig.com, mobile application, or any related services (collectively, the “Platform” and “Services”).

GearGig operates a technology-driven platform that connects customers with independent service providers offering industrial, technical, and business-related services (“Gig Services”).

We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a transparent and secure manner.

By accessing or using the Platform, you agree to the terms of this Policy and consent to the collection and use of your data as described herein.

1. SCOPE AND APPLICABILITY

This Policy applies to all users of the Platform, including customers, visitors, and service providers. References to “you” in this Policy mean any individual or entity that interacts with our Platform or Services.

By using our Platform, you acknowledge and agree that your personal data may be collected, processed, and shared in accordance with this Policy.

We may revise this Policy periodically to reflect updates in our practices, legal requirements, or service offerings. You are encouraged to review this Policy regularly.

2. TYPES OF DATA WE COLLECT

We may collect various categories of personal data, including but not limited to:

(a) Personal Identification Information

This includes your name, phone number, email address, and any other details provided during registration or service booking.

(b) Profile Information

Information such as username, profile photo, gender, and service preferences.

(c) Communication Data

This includes messages, feedback, reviews, chat records, and interactions with customer support or service providers through the Platform.

(d) Technical and Device Information

We may automatically collect data such as IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, access times, and usage patterns when you interact with our Platform.

(e) Transaction Information

Details related to services booked, payment status, transaction history, and limited payment-related data (processed through secure third-party payment providers).

(f) Usage Data

Information about how you navigate and use the Platform, including pages visited, time spent, clicks, and service preferences.

(g) Location Data

Approximate or precise location information may be collected to enable location-based services.

We may also process aggregated or anonymized data that does not directly identify you for analytics and business improvement purposes.

3. HOW WE COLLECT YOUR DATA

We collect your data through the following methods:

(a) Direct Interaction

You provide data when you:

  • Create an account
  • Book services
  • Fill forms or surveys
  • Contact support or provide feedback

(b) Automated Technologies

We automatically collect certain information through cookies, log files, and tracking technologies when you use the Platform.

(c) Third-Party Sources

We may receive data from:

  • Payment partners
  • Analytics providers
  • Service Providers
  • Publicly available sources

4. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use your personal data for purposes including:

  • Creating and managing your account
  • Facilitating service bookings and delivery
  • Connecting you with relevant Service Providers
  • Processing payments and maintaining transaction records
  • Improving platform functionality and user experience
  • Personalizing content and service recommendations
  • Sending notifications related to bookings, updates, or offers
  • Providing customer support
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or misuse
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

We may also use your data for marketing and promotional purposes, subject to your communication preferences.

5. SHARING OF PERSONAL DATA

We may share your data with:

(a) Service Providers

To enable them to deliver the requested services.

(b) Business Partners and Vendors

Including payment gateways, hosting providers, analytics services, and marketing partners.

(c) Affiliates

Companies within the GearGig group for operational purposes.

(d) Legal Authorities

When required by law, regulation, court order, or government authority.

We ensure that third parties receiving your data are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

6. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Remember preferences
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve platform performance

You may choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, although this may affect certain functionalities of the Platform.

7. DATA SECURITY

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration. These measures may include encryption, secure servers, access controls, and monitoring systems.

However, no digital system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

8. DATA RETENTION

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce agreements

Certain data, such as transaction records, may be retained for longer periods as required by law.

9. YOUR RIGHTS

Subject to applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or update
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

You may exercise these rights by contacting us at the email provided below.

10. ACCOUNT DELETION

You may request deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us. Upon verification, we will process your request within a reasonable timeframe, subject to legal retention requirements.

11. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our Platform may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such third parties. You are advised to review their privacy policies separately.

12. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFER

Your data may be stored or processed in locations outside your immediate jurisdiction, including on servers or with partners located in different countries. By using the Platform, you consent to such transfers in compliance with applicable laws.

13. BUSINESS TRANSFERS

In the event of a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of the business transaction, subject to applicable confidentiality safeguards.

14. USER-GENERATED CONTENT

Any content you voluntarily share on the Platform (such as reviews or feedback) may be visible to other users. You are responsible for ensuring that such content does not violate any laws or third-party rights.

15. UPDATES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on the Platform with a revised date. Continued use of the Platform after such updates constitutes your acceptance of the revised Policy.
