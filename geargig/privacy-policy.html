Privacy Policy – GearGig

1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Address/location

b. Service & Usage Data

  • Booking details
  • Service preferences
  • App usage data (logs, interactions)

c. Device & Technical Information

  • Device type
  • IP address
  • Operating system
  • App version

d. Payment Information

  • Transaction ID
  • Payment status
  • Billing details (if applicable)

⚠️ We do NOT store sensitive financial information like card numbers, CVV, or banking credentials.

3. Payment Gateway Compliance

  • All payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways
  • Payment data is encrypted and handled as per PCI-DSS standards
  • We only store limited transaction details for:
    • Order processing
    • Refunds
    • Dispute resolution

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and manage services
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Improve user experience
  • Send notifications (service updates, support, offers)
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share data with:

  • Payment gateway partners
  • Service providers (to fulfill bookings)
  • Analytics tools (for app improvement)

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Safety

  • We collect only necessary data required for app functionality
  • We clearly disclose how data is collected, used, and shared
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data without consent
  • Users can request deletion of their data
  • We request permissions only when required (e.g., location, notifications)
  • Users are informed about why data is collected
  • We ensure transparency in tracking and data usage

7. User Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Access their data
  • Update or correct information
  • Request deletion of their account/data
  • Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at: info@geargig.in

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption
  • Secure servers
  • Restricted access

However, no system is completely secure.

9. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for:

  • Service delivery
  • Legal compliance
  • Dispute resolution

10. Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Improve app performance
  • Analyze user behavior

Users can control cookie settings via their device/browser.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

12. Refunds & Disputes

All payment-related refunds and disputes are handled as per our Refund Policy and may involve third-party payment processors.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes.
